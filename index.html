Cliotris

Controls: ← → ↓ space rotate
Rub four 1x4 pink Clios to clear the entire row or column.
Score: 0
How to Play

This is a Tetris variant where, in addition to the standard pieces, there is a small chance to spawn a special pink 1x4 piece (vertical or horizontal).

To clear rows or columns, you can "rub" (rapid back-and-forth motion) over any pink 1x4 blocks on the grid or the active piece. Rubbing a pink row clears that entire row, and rubbing a pink column clears that entire column.

Use the arrow keys to move and drop pieces, space to hard drop, and Z or Up Arrow to rotate. Try to score as high as possible!
